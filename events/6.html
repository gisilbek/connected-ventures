About This Event

In May 2025, we hosted an informal morning meetup at IdeaLondon led by Wilbe Labs. The event was co-organised by Connected.Ventures and The Entrepreneurs Network and brought together leaders and practitioners shaping the UK's innovation landscape.

Over 30 senior leaders from some of the UK's leading innovation ecosystems attended the event, including representatives from Innovate Finance, Envestors, EISA, KPMG, the Royal Academy of Engineering, and others.

The meetup offered a space for connection, collaboration and conversation with fellow leaders committed to building a more connected and innovative economy.

As part of the event, Natalia Loza had the pleasure of sharing insights on the technical enablement of entrepreneurial ecosystems. Her talk covered the product development approach behind a concept platform designed to help ecosystem leaders form tender consortiums, shared statistics on product success rates across different ecosystems, and offered practical guidance on scalable technical strategies for supporting innovation at the ecosystem level.

Thank you to everyone who joined us for a thoughtful and energising morning.
