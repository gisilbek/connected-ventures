UK Delegation for the Global Entrepreneurship Congress in the US

Event Details

June 2025
Indianapolis, Indiana, USA

About This Event

In June 2025, we partnered with the Global Entrepreneurship Network (GEN) to support the UK delegation at the Global Entrepreneurship Congress (GEC), which took place in Indianapolis, Indiana. This international event brought together thousands of entrepreneurs, investors, policymakers and ecosystem builders from over 200 countries.

Our role included supporting recruitment and coordinating the UK delegation, which featured founders, ecosystem leaders, policy advocates and innovation experts. We also supported the planning and delivery of several key sessions, with a special focus on the Ministerial Room. This was a high-level space where government ministers and public officials from around the world gathered to share ideas and align on startup policy and ecosystem development.

During the event, we presented on the topic of technical enablement—highlighting how digital tools, data platforms and networked infrastructure can support stronger connections among entrepreneurial ecosystems worldwide.

One of the highlights of GEC 2025 was a keynote by entrepreneur and investor Mark Cuban, who spoke about innovation, the future of AI and the global role of entrepreneurship in driving inclusive economic growth. The event also launched GEN's Vision 2035 initiative, which aims to build a more connected and data-driven global ecosystem over the next decade.

The Global Entrepreneurship Congress is now complete. If you're interested in joining the UK delegation at a future GEC or getting involved with GEN's global programmes, feel free to get in touch.
