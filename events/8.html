About This Event

Join us for an informal morning meetup hosted at KPMG. This event is co-organised by Connected.Ventures and The Entrepreneurs Network.

This gathering is for entrepreneurial ecosystem builders – individuals and organisations whose core mission is to drive startup or corporate innovation, support the development of the UK's entrepreneurial ecosystem, and strengthen innovation and technology communities.

Whether you're leading or sponsoring an accelerator, venture builder, corporate innovation team, policymaking body, university innovation office, innovation agency, or other venture support organisation, your work is vital in helping founders and businesses launch, grow, innovate, and succeed.

Come connect over coffee, croissants, and conversation with fellow leaders who believe in technology as a force for change, collaboration, shared success, and building a stronger, more innovative economy.
